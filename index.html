Built with purpose,

SheShield was created because no woman should ever feel alone in a moment of fear. We build technology that bridges the gap between vulnerability and safety — instantly.

Our Mission

Safety should be a right, not a privilege.

SheShield is a real-time women's safety platform that eliminates the delay between distress and assistance. We believe that in critical moments, every second counts — which is why our platform was designed to require just one tap.

We connect women to a verified network of helpers — police officers, female volunteers, and trusted guardians — through intelligent matching that considers proximity, availability, and response history.

🛡️

A Secure Digital Safety Net

Our platform operates 24/7, powered by Firebase real-time infrastructure and smart AI-assisted matching logic to ensure no request goes unanswered.

Our Values

What drives every decision we make

01

Speed

Help must arrive faster than fear. One-click requests and under-60-second matching are non-negotiable standards.

02

Privacy

Identities are protected by default. Anonymous mode, encrypted channels, and minimal data exposure are built in — not bolted on.

03

Trust

Every helper is verified. We don't allow unvetted individuals on our platform — safety is only as strong as the people behind it.

04

Simplicity

In moments of stress, complexity is dangerous. SheShield's interface is deliberately minimal so anyone can use it under pressure.
